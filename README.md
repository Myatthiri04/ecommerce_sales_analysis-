E-Commercne Sales & Customer Anlaytics (SQL Project)

Data Validation & Preparation
Verified total row count (10,000 records)
Checked for missing values across key financial and customer fields
Validated revenue consistency before aggregation
Ensured date formatting for time-series trend analysis

Key Findings
1. Revenue Performance
Total Revenue: $8.09M
Revenue is primarily driven by high-value electronics, with Laptops ($3.17M) and Smartphones ($2.21M) contributing the majority of total sales.
This suggests strong demand concentration in premium tech products.
2. Seasonal Sales Trends
Revenue peaked in:
October 2024 ($737K)
December 2024 ($739K)
Revenue declined in April 2025 ($472K), indicating potential seasonality effects and post-holiday normalization.
Sales volume trends closely mirrored revenue patterns, indicating revenue growth is largely volume-driven.

3. Customer Insights
Revenue is partially concentrated among repeat high-value customers (Top customers each generated $4,800).
Digital payment adoption is evenly distributed, with PayPal leading marginally.
This indicates opportunity for:
-Loyalty programs
-Retention strategies
-Digital checkout optimization

5. Operational Risk
9.6% of total orders (962 orders) were not shipped, representing approximately $733K in unrealized revenue.
Improving fulfillment efficiency could directly increase realized revenue.

##Business Impact Summary##
This analysis demonstrates how SQL-based KPI tracking can uncover:
-Revenue concentration risk
-Seasonal demand patterns
-High-performing product segments
-Customer value distribution
-Operational bottlenecks
